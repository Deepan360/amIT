Business Process Management

Today's economic climate requires constant cost reduction, improved efficiencies, and increased revenues. In order to meet these goals, business process outsourcing (BPO) has increasingly become the preferred solution by leveraging global sourcing models built on global service delivery. Business Process Outsourcing is the long-term contracting out of non-core business processes to an outside provider to help achieve increased shareholder value.

Our experienced BPO specialists located in India provide 7x24x365 support to clients globally. We are committed to building scalable and repeatable business solutions ensuring customer success.

AGS's BPO Services minimize the risks associated with outsourcing by helping you decide which business processes should be outsourced, assigning the right team, structuring your contract, overseeing the work progress, and handling international financial transactions. To ensure seamless communication, we bring you delivery centers that are equipped with the latest software, hardware, and telecommunications equipment.

Shared Services & Outsourcing

The Shared Service and Outsourcing (SSO) model of unifying and centralizing business processes has proven its best ROI for implementing organizations by centralizing and optimizing operations, increasing processing efficiency, improving service standards, and achieving cost savings. An effective SSO must become an innovative business partner to the provider organizations. Meeting these demands requires these organizations to direct capital and focus on globalization, process re-engineering, best practice optimization, analytics, and ongoing innovation. The SSO must be able to meet the objectives of the organization by providing a better vision, ROI, and high-quality skill sets required to achieve this, as many companies are choosing to partner with an SSO provider towards their mission in achieving their targeted growth.

AGS offers an excellent platform in the SSO region that will help our clients achieve:

  • Establishment of SSO Operations
  • Provision of high-end talent with state-of-the-art technologies to run the SSO with the lowest annual attrition rate
  • Refined process in identifying, training, and managing the team with the Workforce Development Model
  • Follow The Sun practice of SSO operations
  • Better costing model with better ROI
  • Continuous introduction of innovations, best practices, and enabling technologies, to deliver ongoing services
  • Risk management, mitigation, and regulatory compliance focus
